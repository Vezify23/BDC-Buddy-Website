How It Works

Install in 2 minutes. No IT department needed.

1

Install the Chrome Extension

Download the zip, unpack it, and load it in Chrome developer mode. Takes 60 seconds. Works on any computer with Chrome + DriveCentric access.

2

Sign In & Set Your Name

Create your account with your work email. BDC Buddy uses your name and dealership in every message so they sound like you wrote them.

3

Open DriveCentric & Hit Start

Navigate to your work plan, orphans, or leads queue. Click Start (with approval) or Auto (hands-free). BDC Buddy handles the rest.

4

Watch Your Numbers Climb

Check the Stats tab to see your daily output — texts, emails, send rate, and by-mode breakdown. Do in 10 minutes what used to take all day.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is BDC Buddy free?
Yes — completely free during the beta. We'll introduce pricing later but early users will always get a discount.
What CRM does it work with?
BDC Buddy is built specifically for DriveCentric. It runs as a Chrome extension that works directly inside the DriveCentric interface — no switching tabs, no copy-pasting.
Is my data safe?
BDC Buddy never stores customer data on our servers. Messages are generated in real-time and sent directly through DriveCentric. We only log your outreach stats (counts, not content) for the analytics dashboard.
Will my manager know I'm using AI?
Messages are personalized with your name and dealership info — they read like you wrote them. Your stats will go up and your customers will get better follow-ups. That's all your manager will see.
Do I need IT to install it?
Nope. It's a Chrome extension you install yourself in 60 seconds. No admin access, no software downloads, no IT tickets.
Can I review messages before they send?
Absolutely. The default mode shows you each message for approval. You can edit it, skip it, or approve it. Auto mode sends without asking — you pick which one you want per session.
